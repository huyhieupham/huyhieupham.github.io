Huy Hieu Pham

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at the Toulouse Computer Science Research Institute (IRIT), France and Cerema Research Center. My research focuses on Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. In particular, I design and optimize high-performance deep learning networks for solving the security problems in public transport, e.g. the problem of human action recognition and behavior analysis in videos.

At Cerema and IRIT, I was advised by Louahdi Khoudour, Alain Crouzil, Pablo Zegers, and Sergio A. Velastin. From November 2015 to May 2016, I have done a research internship at the ICA laboratory, Ecole des Mines d'Albi. My work is part of a dynamic multi-partner robotized airplane inspection project, called Air-Cobot , lead by AKKA Technologies and AIRBUS group.

Previously, I did my bachelors at the Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST). My graduate internship was done at International Research Institute MICA, Hanoi, Vietnam et AGIM laboratory, Université Grenoble Alpes, Grenoble, France. The main goal of this project is to develop a computer vision algorithm based on electrode matrix and mobile Kinect for detecting obstacles and warning to visually impaired people.
